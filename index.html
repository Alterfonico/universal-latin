About Universal Latin Limo

At Universal Latin Limo & Tours, we are dedicated to providing unparalleled luxury ground transportation services tailored specifically for music artists and celebrities.

With over 25-years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as one of the most trusted and reliable partner available, ensuring our esteemed clients experience the epitome of elegance, comfort, and professionalism.

Our meticulously maintained fleet of top-of-the-line vehicles, driven by highly trained chauffeurs, guarantees a seamless and prestigious travel experience. We take pride in our unwavering commitment to discretion, privacy, and exceeding our clients' expectations.
